---

## Selection framework

Every company was screened against these signals. Tier 1 companies clear most of them; Tier 2 (bench) companies clear several but have a gap or an unverified signal.

| Signal | Why it matters | Where to check |
|---|---|---|
| Growth (GTM headcount ↑, revenue ↑) | A company adding salespeople is a company where sales is working | LinkedIn Insights, funding news |
| Profitability / funding runway | No mid-quarter layoff surprises | Tracxn, press, annual filings |
| G2 momentum (not just rating) | A loved product is an easier quota | G2 Grid movement, review growth |
| Glassdoor — **sales dept filter** | Overall rating hides sales-org reality | Glassdoor, filter by department |
| RepVue score & quota attainment | Sales-specific: OTE accuracy, % of reps hitting quota | repvue.com |
| Layoff history (18–24 mo) | Especially GTM layoffs | layoffs.fyi, TechCrunch tracker |
| Sales motion fit | Outbound-led orgs value BDR leadership; enterprise ACV = higher OTE | Job posts, org structure |
| Actively hiring GTM in India | Warmest signal for outreach | Careers page, LinkedIn |
| Rep tenure | High sales churn = loudest red flag | LinkedIn people tab |
| ESOP quality | Buyback history vs. paper equity | Press (buyback announcements) |

**Comp benchmarks (India, 2026):** AE average ≈ ₹24L; top 10% of AEs > ₹45L; Enterprise AE median OTE ≈ ₹65L (RepVue). Rippling India average total comp ≈ ₹59L (6figr); Rippling SDR median base ≈ ₹14L (Glassdoor). Your ₹29L is beatable at most Tier 1 companies in an AE or SDR-manager seat.

**Market context (mid-2026):** Indian SaaS growing ~24% CAGR; 250+ Indian companies past $10M ARR; $1.26B raised across 103 rounds by April 2026 (+38% YoY). Counter-current: AI-driven layoffs at mega-vendors (Salesforce, Oracle, Meta ad-sales) hit India delivery/GTM centers in 2026 — which is why this list favors mid-stage growth companies over legacy giants.

- **Watch:** famously intense pace; make peace with the 3-days-in-office hybrid and high-accountability culture before applying.
- **Reach:** Head of Sales Development (India), India GTM site lead. **Angle:** your outbound playbook + team-leadership story maps 1:1 to their outbound SMB motion.

- **Why:** one of the fastest-growing software companies ever (AI tailwind), 3,000+ India jobs listed, has run India-market BDR hiring. Pre-IPO equity is a real wealth event possibility.

## How to work this list

1. **Priority order within Tier 1:** start where a live opening matches your role (Rippling, Whatfix, Snowflake today), then warm the rest before roles open — the best BDR-manager hires happen before the JD is public.
2. **Per-company 15-minute diligence before outreach:** RepVue score, Glassdoor filtered to sales, LinkedIn GTM headcount 6-month trend, last funding/layoff news.
3. **Who to write to:** India-HQ → founder or VP Sales/CRO directly; global → Head of Sales Development India or Country Manager. CC-nobody, short note, one proof point, one ask.
4. **Your angle is the list itself:** you built a signal-scored target list with a research pipeline — that *is* the BDR-manager job talk. Show it.
